Social Media
Management

Your brand's daily voice online, handled properly. Content calendars, captions, post design, community management — turning your social channels into a consistent lead generation machine.

Content StrategyCommunity ManagementGrowthAnalyticsBrand Voice
Why most brand
pages look
abandoned.

Most businesses post when someone remembers to. No strategy, no consistency, no clear idea of what each post is supposed to achieve. The result is a page that looks abandoned — even if there's technically content on it. Customers notice that, and it costs trust before they ever message you.

We treat social media as a system, not a chore. Every post has a purpose — building trust, demonstrating expertise, driving a specific action — and it goes out on a consistent schedule your audience can rely on. We also handle the unglamorous part: responding to comments and messages so nothing falls through the cracks.

Our Process

How we take your
pages from quiet to consistent

01
Day 1 — Discovery Call

We learn your brand voice and goals

We ask what you want social media to actually do for your business — brand awareness, direct leads, customer trust before a sale. We also review your current pages, past content, and what's resonated (or hasn't) before.

You get: A clear definition of what success on social media actually looks like for your business.
02
Day 2-4 — Audit & Content Pillars

We map out what to post and why

We audit your competitors and your existing audience, then define 3-5 content pillars — recurring themes that build trust and showcase your offer. Every future post maps back to one of these pillars, so nothing is random.

You get: A documented content strategy showing exactly what kinds of posts we'll run and why.
03
Day 5-7 — First Content Calendar

We build the first month's content

Captions, post designs, and a posting schedule for the upcoming month — built around your content pillars and key dates relevant to your business. You review and approve before anything goes live.

You get: A full content calendar to review, with room for your feedback before it's locked in.
04
Ongoing — Daily Execution

Consistent posting, every week

Posts go out on schedule across the platforms you've chosen. We monitor and respond to comments and DMs so your audience always gets a reply — not silence that makes the page look unmanaged.

You get: A page that always looks active, current, and responsive — exactly what builds trust before someone buys.
05
Every week — Performance Check

We watch what's working and adjust

We track which posts get the most engagement, saves, and shares — then lean into those formats and topics. Content that flops gets dropped quickly instead of repeated out of habit.

You get: A strategy that keeps evolving based on real performance, not guesswork.
06
Monthly — Report & Review

We show you the growth, honestly

Monthly reports covering follower growth, reach, engagement rate, and top-performing content — plus a short call to plan next month's content direction together.

You get: Clear visibility into whether your social presence is actually growing month over month.

FAQ

Social media questions

How many posts per week do you create?

This depends on your package — typically 3-5 feed posts per week plus Stories, across the platforms you choose. We agree on exact frequency during onboarding based on your goals and budget.

Do I need to approve every post before it goes live?

Yes, especially in the first month. You'll get the content calendar in advance to review and approve. As trust builds, many clients move to a lighter review process if they prefer.

Will this directly bring me sales?

Social media management builds trust and visibility — it's rarely a direct sales channel on its own. For direct leads and sales, we usually pair this with paid ads or our lead generation service.

Can you take over an existing page with a posting history?

Yes. We'll audit what's there, keep what's working, and rebuild the strategy around your existing audience rather than starting from zero.
