Google Ads

Capture people who are already searching for what you sell. Search, Shopping, Display and YouTube campaigns built to put you in front of high-intent buyers at the right moment.

Searching for
you is the highest
intent there is.

When someone types "buy running shoes Karachi" or "best dentist near me," they're not browsing — they're ready to act. Google Ads puts your business in front of that exact intent, but only if the keyword strategy, ad copy, and landing page all line up correctly.

We don't just bid on broad keywords and hope. We structure campaigns around real search intent, write ad copy that matches what people are actually looking for, and constantly trim wasted spend on searches that look relevant but never convert.

Our Process

How we take you from
first call to running campaigns

01
Day 1 — Discovery Call

We understand what you sell and who searches for it

A free 30-minute call covering your product, competitors, current Google presence, and your average order value or service price. This tells us whether Search, Shopping, or a mix makes sense for your business.

You get: A clear recommendation on which Google Ads campaign types fit your business.
02
Day 2-3 — Keyword Research & Audit

We find exactly what your customers are typing

Deep keyword research split into match types and intent levels, plus a negative keyword list to stop budget leaking on irrelevant searches. If you've run Google Ads before, we audit the account first to see what's been wasted.

You get: A full keyword list with search volume, competition, and estimated cost per click.
03
Day 4-5 — Conversion Tracking Setup

We make sure every result is actually measured

Google Tag, conversion actions for purchases/form-fills/calls, and Google Analytics 4 linked properly. Without this step, you can't trust any number the campaign reports — so we never skip it.

You get: Accurate tracking so we can prove exactly what your spend produced.
04
Day 6-7 — Campaign Build & Launch

Campaigns go live

Ad groups structured by intent and product category, compelling ad copy written for each, sitelink and callout extensions added. We launch with tight daily budgets first to control spend while we observe initial performance.

You get: Live search campaigns plus dashboard access to watch impressions, clicks, and conversions.
05
Week 2-4 — Search Term Mining

We cut waste and double down on what converts

Weekly review of the actual search terms triggering your ads — adding new negative keywords, pausing underperforming ad groups, and increasing bids on terms that are converting well.

You get: Weekly reports showing cost per click, cost per conversion, and what changed.
06
Ongoing — Scale & Expand

We grow into Shopping, Display, and YouTube

Once Search is performing reliably, we layer in Shopping ads for product visibility, Display for retargeting, or YouTube for awareness — depending on what makes sense for your growth stage.

You get: A Google Ads presence that expands strategically, not randomly.

FAQ

Google Ads questions

How is Google Ads different from Meta Ads?

Google captures people actively searching for a solution — high intent, ready to act. Meta reaches people who aren't searching yet but match your ideal customer profile. Many businesses benefit from running both together.

What's the minimum budget for Google Ads?

We recommend at least Rs. 40,000/month in ad spend, separate from our fee. Google Ads in competitive industries can have higher cost per click than Meta, so budget needs to be sufficient to gather data.

Can you fix an existing account that's underperforming?

Yes, this is one of our most common starting points. We audit the account, identify exactly where budget has been wasted, and rebuild the structure where needed.

Do you handle Google Shopping for e-commerce?

Yes. We set up and optimize your product feed through Google Merchant Center and manage Shopping campaigns alongside Search if it fits your business.
