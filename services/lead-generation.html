Stop chasing cold leads. We build complete funnels — ad to landing page to follow-up — that deliver qualified, ready-to-buy leads directly to you, tracked and optimized for the lowest possible cost.

A lead is worthless
without the right
system behind it.

Most businesses treat lead generation as just running an ad and hoping the phone rings. The reality is leads die fast — if nobody follows up within minutes, the chance of converting drops sharply with every hour that passes. A great ad with a broken follow-up system is just a wasted budget.

We build the entire system, not just the ad. That means a landing page built to convert, a form that captures the right information, and automated follow-up that reaches the lead before they've even closed the tab. Every lead is tracked from click to close so you know exactly what your money is producing.

Our Process

How we build a funnel
that actually converts

01
Day 1 — Discovery Call

We map your ideal customer and offer

We learn what a qualified lead actually looks like for your business — budget range, urgency, specific needs — so we don't just generate volume, we generate leads worth your sales team's time.

You get: A clear definition of what "qualified" means for your business, agreed before we build anything.
02
Day 2-4 — Landing Page & Form

We build a page designed to convert, not impress

A dedicated landing page with one clear offer and one clear action — not your homepage with ten distractions. The form asks only what's needed to qualify the lead, with built-in qualifying questions to filter out time-wasters.

You get: A purpose-built page for this offer, plus a form that pre-qualifies leads before they ever reach you.
03
Day 5-6 — Automation Setup

We build the instant follow-up system

The moment someone submits the form, an automated WhatsApp message or email goes out within seconds — not hours later when someone remembers to check the inbox. We also connect leads to your CRM or a simple tracking sheet if you don't have one.

You get: Leads contacted automatically within minutes, before they've moved on to a competitor.
04
Day 7 — Launch

Ads go live driving traffic to the funnel

We launch the ad campaign feeding the funnel — Meta, Google, or both, depending on where your customers actually are. Every click, form submission, and lead source is tracked from this point on.

You get: A live, working funnel generating leads with full tracking on cost per lead.
05
Week 2-4 — Optimize the Funnel

We fix the leaks, not just the ad

If cost per lead is high or lead quality is poor, we don't just tweak the ad — we look at the whole funnel. Sometimes the form is too long, sometimes the offer needs sharpening, sometimes the targeting is off. We diagnose and fix the actual problem.

You get: A funnel that keeps improving, not one that's "set and forgotten" after launch.
06
Ongoing — Report & Scale

We show you cost per lead and what it became

Weekly reports showing not just lead volume, but cost per qualified lead and — where you share it back with us — how many of those leads actually became sales. We scale budget once we know the funnel is genuinely converting.

You get: Full visibility connecting ad spend to real leads to real revenue, not just a lead counter.

FAQ

Lead generation questions

What's the difference between this and just running ads?

Running ads sends traffic somewhere. Lead generation is the entire system — the offer, the landing page, the form, the instant follow-up, and the tracking. Ads alone without this system waste a huge percentage of the leads they generate.

How fast does the follow-up actually happen?

Within seconds to a couple of minutes of someone submitting the form, depending on the automation platform we use for your setup. Speed of follow-up is one of the biggest factors in whether a lead actually converts.

Do you handle the sales calls or follow-up myself?

We handle the automated first contact and lead delivery. Closing the sale is on your team — we make sure they get a warm, qualified lead quickly so they have the best possible chance to convert it.

What budget do I need for a lead generation campaign?

This depends on your industry and lead value, but we typically recommend starting with at least Rs. 40,000/month in ad spend to gather enough volume to optimize the funnel properly.
