FAQ

Pricing questions

Is the ad spend included in your fee?

No. Our management fee covers our strategy, setup, and optimization work. Your ad spend budget goes directly to Meta, Google, or TikTok and is separate — you control that budget and can adjust it anytime.

What's the minimum contract length?

We recommend a minimum of 3 months because ad campaigns need time to gather data and optimize properly. After that, it's month-to-month — no long-term lock-in contracts.

Can I switch packages later?

Yes. Most clients start with Starter or Growth and move up once they see results and want to scale further. We'll recommend the right time to upgrade based on your data.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes. If your needs don't fit neatly into these three packages, book a call and we'll build something specific to your business and budget.
