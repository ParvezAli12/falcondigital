How It Works

From first call to
first results — fast.

We get campaigns live within 48 hours of signing. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you reach out to the moment your phone starts ringing.

01
Day 1

Discovery Call — Free, 30 Minutes

We learn your business: your product, your customers, your goals, your budget, and who you're competing against. No pitch decks. No sales scripts. Just a real conversation about whether we're the right fit — and if we're not, we'll tell you that too.

You get: An honest assessment of your current ad situation and what we'd do differently
02
Day 2–3

Custom Strategy — Built For Your Business

Our team builds your complete ad strategy: which platforms, which audiences, what creative angles to test, how to structure the budget, and what success looks like in week 1 vs month 3. Everything is specific to you — nothing is copy-pasted from another client.

You get: A full campaign strategy document with platform plan, audience breakdown, and creative brief
03
Day 4–5

Launch — Campaigns Go Live

We set up your ad accounts, build the creatives, write the copy, configure targeting, install tracking pixels, and launch. From here we're watching your campaigns daily — adjusting bids, pausing underperformers, scaling what's working.

You get: Live campaigns running and access to a real-time dashboard showing your results
04
Ongoing

Scale — We Double Down on What Works

Every week we analyze your data, kill what isn't performing, and pour more budget into what is. Every month you get a full report. Every quarter we revisit strategy. The longer we work together, the better your results get — because we know your business deeper every day.

You get: Weekly updates, monthly strategy reviews, and a partner who grows with your business
